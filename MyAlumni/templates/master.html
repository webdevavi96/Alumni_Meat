About Institute

Mahamaya IT Polytechnic Maharajganj is a Government diploma college located in Dhanewa-Dhanei, Maharajganj, Uttar Pradesh. Affiliated with the Uttar Pradesh Board of Technical Education, the college offers full-time, AICTE-approved diploma courses in various engineering fields.

The available programs include:

  • Diploma in Computer Science and Engineering (1st shift) - Maximum intake: 75
  • Diploma in Electrical Engineering (1st shift) - Maximum intake: 75
  • Diploma in Electronic Engineering (1st shift) - Maximum intake: 75

All courses are designed to provide quality education and training in specific technical disciplines, preparing students for careers in the rapidly evolving engineering and technology sectors.
